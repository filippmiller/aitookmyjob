# Improvement Plans — All 4 Projects
## Generated 2026-04-06 by TL based on competitive analysis

---

## PROJECT 1: AITOOKMYJOB — "Beat Layoffs.fyi with human stories"

### Critical Gap: Empty content = dead on arrival
**Priority 1: Seed 50+ realistic AI displacement stories**
- Generate diverse stories: tech workers, creatives, translators, customer service, manufacturing
- Each story: name, avatar, job title, company type, how AI replaced them, what they did next, emotional journey
- Mix languages: 60% EN, 15% RU, 10% DE, 10% FR, 5% ES
- Include "success" stories (people who pivoted) — not just doom

**Priority 2: Live data dashboard**
- Add real layoff statistics (scrape public data or use API)
- Show "X people affected this week" counter
- Industry breakdown chart in sidebar

**Priority 3: Onboarding flow that converts**
- "Your story matters" → 3-step wizard: What happened? → How do you feel? → What's next?
- Anonymous option (lower barrier)
- Social proof: "Join 847 others sharing their experience"

**Priority 4: Community features**
- Forum categories: By industry, By country, Support groups, Job leads
- Upvote/react to stories
- Weekly digest email

## PROJECT 2: STREET — "Own Bangkok nightlife, not just one road"

### Critical Gap: Single-street guide has no reason to exist
**Priority 1: Expand scope to "Bangkok After Dark" or "Bangkok Street Life"**
- Rename from Khao San-only to Bangkok nightlife/street culture guide
- Add 5 more areas: Sukhumvit, Silom, Thonglor, Chinatown, Rambuttri
- Keep Khao San as the flagship/deepest section

**Priority 2: Real-time & interactive content**
- Live event calendar (what's happening tonight?)
- Price checker: "How much should a pad thai cost here?"
- Safety tips with real-time updates
- "Ask a local" feature

**Priority 3: Original content that Atlas Obscura can't match**
- Hidden gems section: "Things only locals know"
- Photo stories from street vendors
- Audio ambiance recordings (already have AmbientSoundscape island!)
- Street food guide with allergy warnings

**Priority 4: Monetization angle**
- Affiliate links to tours/hostels/experiences
- "Skip the line" premium tips
- Downloadable offline guide (PWA)

## PROJECT 3: NOMAD — "96 cafes, verified better than Nomads.com"

### Critical Gap: Scale mismatch (96 vs 411K check-ins)
**Priority 1: Make every cafe entry the BEST on the internet**
- For each cafe: wifi speed (tested), outlet count, noise level, coffee quality rating, price range
- Photos: workspace setup, menu, exterior, outlet locations
- "Best for" tags: video calls, deep work, meetings, casual, dates
- Opening hours verified within last 30 days

**Priority 2: City guides that Nomads.com lacks**
- "Best cafe for X in Istanbul/Rome/Lisbon" curated lists
- Day-in-the-life itineraries: "Work from Istanbul: A 7-day plan"
- Cost comparison: "Istanbul vs Lisbon for remote workers"

**Priority 3: Community data layer**
- "Confirm this info" button on each cafe (crowdsource freshness)
- User reviews with wifi speed verification
- "I'm here now" check-in (show active nomads)

**Priority 4: SEO & content marketing**
- Blog: "Top 10 cafes in [city] for remote work 2026"
- Each cafe page: unique meta description, schema.org markup
- City landing pages optimized for "best coworking cafe [city]"

---

## PROJECT 4: LOCALSDOIT — "Own one city, one service category"

### Critical Gap: Can't compete with Thumbtack's $3B ecosystem
**Priority 1: Pick a niche and dominate it**
- Focus: ONE city (e.g., Bangkok) + ONE category (e.g., home cleaning, or language tutoring)
- 50+ verified providers in that niche
- Every provider: photo, bio, pricing, availability calendar, reviews

**Priority 2: Trust signals that Thumbtack took years to build**
- Provider verification badges (ID verified, background check, insured)
- Customer protection guarantee (even if symbolic: "$100 satisfaction guarantee")
- Transparent pricing: no hidden fees, price lock after booking

**Priority 3: UX that's simpler than Thumbtack**
- 3-click booking: Search → Pick provider → Book (not Thumbtack's 15-question flow)
- Instant messaging with providers
- Real-time availability (not "request a quote and wait")

**Priority 4: Local advantage**
- Support local currencies and payment methods
- Multi-language support already built (leverage it!)
- Cultural context: reviews and descriptions in local language + English
